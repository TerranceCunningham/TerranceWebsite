LOGO DESIGN

Project: Dawnologist Logo Design Study.

While meeting up several times with my client to discuss the logo name she decided that "Phyllis's Wonderful World Of Dawn" would fit her style the best, After figuring out what the logo name was going to be I started with my type face studies and found a font that my client liked and then created the logo in Adobe Photoshop.

COLOR SCHEME

Project: Dawnologist Vintage Doll Color Scheme.

The client requested that I go with a loud and bright Color Scheme to match the Dawn Doll company theme. While researching Dawn Dolls and meeting up with my client to discuss the best colors that would match the site we decided to go with two palettes.

WIREFRAMING

Project: Dawnologist Wireframing and Layout.

While creating Dawnolgist's Wireframes I implemented a 3 column grid system. For the Informational pages I added a left sidebar for easy access, during this process I created a number system for my client to follow along with the different sections of the site each number represented where a section would be.
