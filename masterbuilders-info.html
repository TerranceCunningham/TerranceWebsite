Masterbuilder's A Wordpress Studies.

In my Wordpress class at Seattle Central Community College, I developed using Wordpress a website with a blogging system, calander section, recent and latest posts area or page. As I organized Images and themes for wordress I also met up with my client to discuss any additions or changes that needed to be done.

COLOR SCHEME

Project: Masterbuilder's Color Scheme.

I chose dark brown, light blue and gray for Masterbuilder's color scheme it makes for a clean orginazed look and feel. Masterbuilder's Color Scheme is based from the different images that I took while viewing my clients live work sites and taking photographs of his houses that he had built.

VISUAL DESIGN

Project: Masterbuilder's Visual Look And Feel.

With Masterbuilder's Color Scheme painted over the top of the wireframes you really get the feel of a construction site. Masterbuilders Color Scheme stays consistent throughout the site for an overall clean look.

WP STUDIES

Project: Masterbuilder's is built as a Wordpress Study.

Before intergrating Masterbuilder's onto Wordpress I needed to create an account for my client so he could edit his site freely. After creating the account my next step was to find a theme and integrate the site to that theme.

WP-WIDGET STUDIES

Project: Masterbuilder's Widget Creation And Studies

After Masterbuilder's was integrated to Wordpress I needed to then build the sites accessories widgets for my clients to be able to change the site around at his own convience.
