Since 1907

Located in the little town of Poulsbo, our hardware store is locally owned and operated by Bonita Doerksen, her eldest daughter, Nicole and wonderful staff. Bonita and Brian bought Coast to Coast Hardware in 1995. After losing Brian in 2002, Bonita decided to keep the business and with the support of her daughter and long term staff, the hardware store is thriving.


We pride ourselves on wonderful customer service and customer camaraderie. Our customers are often quoted saying, "We always come here first." "We love coming into your store for the personal help and friendly smiles." "You guys always have what I need!"
