Coast Do It Best Hardware Re-Design Look And Feel.

Owner and operators, Nick and Nicole of Coast Do It Best Hardware located in Pousblo, WA requested a redesign of their existing website. They were looking for a visual appealing and easily navigated website. During our initial consultation we reviewed their web needs and assessed their current site. The site was unorganized with poor navigation including faulty links. View Site Template

COMPARISON

Project: Coast Do It Best Hardware Comparison

On the original website there were repeated images and unnecessary white space which created a visually unappealing site. Overall the website provided a frustrating shopping experience and neglected to offer customer interaction.

COLOR SCHEME

Project: Coast Do It Best Hardware Color Scheme used to color and liven up site.

Coast Do It Best Hardware website redesign involved choosing color schemes, developing graphics, and organizing the flow of the website. Nick and Nicole decided that their old site was just to plain and had no color in it, so the color scheme that they wanted to go with for their new site would be bright but also have dark colors in it as well.

WIREFRAMING

Project: Coast Do It Best Hardware Wireframe displaying the structure and layout of how the site will flow.

The website design allows for expansion and business growth without impacting the organization and visual appeal of the site. Designing a new product upload procedure for the owners helped to streamline the process and maintain the structural integrity of the site

VISUAL DESIGN

Project: Coast Do It Best Hardware Visual Design shows the sites look and feel.

To develop visual appeal, I created a crisp color scheme, removed excessive white space and added a feature spotlight. These elements work together to create visual flow. View Site Template
